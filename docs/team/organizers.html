Organizing Team

At DataPhilly, our dedicated organizers work tirelessly to bring you the best events, workshops, and networking opportunities. Here's the team behind the scenes:

Miguel Di Tolla​

  • Role: Lead Organizer
  • Bio: Miguel has over a decade of experience in data science and machine learning. He oversees all DataPhilly events and ensures they run smoothly.

  • Linkedin: Miguel Di Tolla

Marc Leprince​

  • Role: Partnerships: Sponsorships & Event Spaces co-ordinator
  • Bio: Marc began coding SAS over 10 years ago, and eventually joined as a consultant and worked as an architect, and pre-sales engineer. Now he works as an independent consultant (Bambou Solutions) helping banks deploy analytics platforms and run models at scale to generate management and regulatory reports. Talk to Marc if you have a space to host an event or would like to sponsor DataPhilly!

Tetyana Yatsenko​

  • Role: Community Engagement and Study group co-ordinator

Currently a Data Scientist in Finance, she is passionate about research and innovation. At DataPhilly, she leads community engagement on social media and coordinates study groups.
  • Linkedin: Tetyana Yatsenko

Shekar Venkateswaran​
